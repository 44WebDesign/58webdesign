Our Story & Ethos

Mr Bees Brewery started with a simple hive and a big dream. We believe in independent brewing that respects the environment and supports local ecosystems. By using Felixstowe honey in every batch, we're not just making beer; we're supporting the local bee population and celebrating the unique flavors of our coastal home.
