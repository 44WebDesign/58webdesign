What We Do
Our Services
01
MOT Testing

Class IV MOT testing for cars up to 8 passenger seats, dual purpose vehicles, and vans up to 3000kg. DVSA approved station with experienced testers on-site.

02
Full Servicing

Complete vehicle servicing for all makes and models. Oil changes, filter replacements, fluid checks, and brake inspections — all recorded in your service book.

03
Vehicle Repairs

From minor fixes to major mechanical repairs, our fully qualified technicians handle everything at highly competitive rates. You only pay for what your car needs.

04
MOT Repairs

Failed your MOT elsewhere? We carry out all repair work needed to get your vehicle back on the road quickly and affordably. Transparent pricing, always.

05
Diagnostics

Modern diagnostic equipment to identify faults quickly and accurately. We get to the root of the problem before any work begins, saving you time and money.

06
Collection & Delivery

Can't get to us? We can collect and return your vehicle by prior arrangement. Available across Felixstowe and surrounding areas for your convenience.
